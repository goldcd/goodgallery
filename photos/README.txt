GoodGallery Photos
==================

Place your photos in this directory.
The AI will automatically tag them when you start the application.

Supported formats: JPG, JPEG, PNG, GIF, WebP

Quick Start:
1. Copy your photos into this folder
2. Run launcher.bat (Windows) or ./launcher.sh (Unix/Mac)
3. Open http://localhost:5000 in your browser
4. Watch as your photos are automatically tagged!
